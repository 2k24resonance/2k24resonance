The Gandhigram Rural Institute (DTBU), Gandhigram

The Gandhigram Rural Institute of Higher Education was founded in 1956 to carry on Mahatma Gandhi's "Nai Talim" system of education. In 1976, it was declared as Deemed University by UGC, New Delhi. This Institute was founded by Dr.T.S. Soundaram and Dr.G. Ramachandran. The institute is accredited with 5-star status by NAAC , in 2002. It is re-accredited with "A grade" in 2010 and "A"(3rd cycle) grade re-accredited in 2016.

Department of Chemistry

In 1976, the Department started the B.Sc. Programme in Chemistry with Physics and Mathematics as allied subjects. The Department started offering M.Sc. in Applied Chemistry from 1987 to 1994, and this program was renamed as M.Sc. Chemistry in 1994. The Department started admitting students to the Ph.D. program in 1991 and M.Phil. Chemistry from the academic year 2007-08.
